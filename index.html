About

Architecture, light, and emotional precision.

Edem Seitasanov is an architect-artist whose work exists at the intersection of structure and emotion. Holding a PhD in Architecture and Urban Planning from the National Academy of Fine Arts and Architecture in Kyiv, his artistic language is informed by spatial thinking, compositional balance, and an understanding of how light shapes perception.

Years of architectural research refined his sensitivity to proportion, rhythm, and silence within form. Each canvas becomes a dialogue between precision and intuition, where geometry meets atmosphere. Beyond painting, Edem is also a composer — music and visual art coexist through tempo, tension, and harmony.

Every work at Edem ArtHouse is a singular creation — one-of-one, original, and unrepeated. There are no reproductions or editions. Each painting is conceived as a complete artistic statement for collectors who value authenticity and depth.
