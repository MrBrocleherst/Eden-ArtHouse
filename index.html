About

Edem Seitasanov is an architect-artist whose work exists at the intersection of structure and emotion. Holding a PhD in Architecture and Urban Planning from the National Academy of Fine Arts and Architecture in Kyiv, his artistic language is deeply informed by spatial thinking, compositional balance, and an understanding of how light shapes perception. His paintings are not spontaneous gestures — they are constructed experiences.

Years of architectural research have refined his sensitivity to proportion, rhythm, and silence within form. Each canvas becomes a dialogue between precision and intuition, where geometry meets atmosphere. Beyond painting, Edem is also a composer and creative visionary. Music and visual art coexist in his practice, influencing one another through tempo, tension, and harmony. The same discipline that defines architectural space informs his brushwork; the same emotional depth found in sound resonates through his color palette.

Every work at Eden ArtHouse is a singular creation — one-of-one, original, and unrepeated. There are no reproductions, no editions. Each painting is conceived as a complete artistic statement, intended for collectors who value authenticity, depth, and intellectual craftsmanship. Through light, structure, and quiet emotional intensity, Edem creates art that inhabits space — not merely decorates it.
